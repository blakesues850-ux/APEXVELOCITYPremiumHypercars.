Tournament Regulations

1. Competitive Integrity

Any form of hardware alteration, third-party software exploitation, or collusion will result in immediate disqualification without appeal.

2. Pauses & Technicals

Each team is permitted a maximum of two (2) tactical pauses per map, lasting no longer than 60 seconds each. Technical pauses must clear admin authorization.

3. Format Structure

Group stages follow a round-robin format. Playoffs scale to a single-elimination bracket setup. Regular matches are Bo3, Grand Finals run as Bo5.
